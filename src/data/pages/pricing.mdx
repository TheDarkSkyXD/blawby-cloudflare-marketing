## Frequently asked Questions

### How are payment fees calculated?

Fees are calculated by payment method (card or ACH) and applied per successful transaction.

### Are there additional fees for chargebacks on card transactions?

Blawby charges a $30 fee for disputed payments (also known as chargebacks) on card transactions.

### Do you have setup fees or monthly fees?

There are no setup fees or hidden closure fees. Standard plans include a $40/month per active user fee plus transaction fees listed on this page.

### Do you offer discounts?

Blawby offers custom pricing for businesses with large processing volumes. Eligibility varies by market.
